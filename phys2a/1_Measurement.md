# Measurement

#### International System of Units
|Quantity|Unit Name|Unit Symbol|
|---|---|---|
|Length| meter| m|
|Time| second| s|
|Mass|kilogram| kg|

- SI derived units are defined in terms of these base units (ex. SI unit for power -> watt)
$$ 1 \text{watt} = 1\text{kg}\cdot m^2/s^3$$
